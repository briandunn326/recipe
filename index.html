Breadsticks Recipe

    Ingredients Needed:

  • For the dough:

  • 8 ounces bread flour (1 cup)
  • 1.5 teaspoons granulated sugar
  • 1.5 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 5/8 cups ice water
  • 1.5 teaspoons vegetable/mixed oil

  • For the garlic butter:

  • Melted butter
  • Garlic powder to taste

    Cooking Instructions

  1. Make a dome in the flour and pour water and all seasoning and oil into the flour
  2. Mix the flour together with the contents until it starts to form dough
  3. Knead dough until it is smooth and then place in an oiled bowl overnight
  4. Deflate the dough and slice dough into breadstick-sized portions
  5. Place breadsticks in an oven at 450Â°F until they have browned adequately
  6. Take the breadsticks out after around 10 minutes and allow the breadsticks to cool down
  7. Brush with garlic butter and sprinkle parmesan on top

Backstory

I am chose this recipe to share because it is what I am most familiar with since I used to work at an Italian restaurant. It is a time-consuming recipe, but the dough it produces has no contender that I have found other than where I used to work or an actual pizzeria in New York. The dough can be used for multiple things as a base, such as a pizza (what it is actually meant for), breadsticks, donuts, or whatever you can think of that you need good dough for.

Allergins

Gluten is used in this recipe for the dough, if you have any sensitivity to garlic I would skip the garlic butter part. Skip the parmesan if you do not like parmesan cheese or have problems with dairy. The dough is rather sugary, diabetics beware.
